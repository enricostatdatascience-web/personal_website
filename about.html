About

  • Location: Putnam, Connecticut
  • School: University of Connecticut
  • Degree: B.S. Statistical Data Science
  • Graduation: May 2028

I am a Statistical Data Science student at the University of Connecticut with a strong interest in applying data-driven methods to real-world problems, particularly in finance, technology, and sports analytics. As a first-generation Filipino-Chinese immigrant from Putnam, Connecticut, my background has shaped my perspective on problem-solving, adaptability, and the value of opportunity, all of which influence how I approach data and decision-making.

Technical foundation

My academic experience has given me a solid foundation in statistical modeling, data analysis, and programming, with hands-on work in Python and R to clean, analyze, and visualize complex datasets. I have built regression models, conducted exploratory data analysis, and interpreted results to generate meaningful insights that support decision-making.

Practical experience

Alongside my coursework, I have gained practical experience through roles in data analysis and IT support. In my current position supporting Athletics IT and device management at UConn, I troubleshoot technical issues, manage ticketing systems, and work with enterprise tools to improve system efficiency and user experience. Previously, I worked as a Data Analyst, where I collected and analyzed regional data to identify socioeconomic patterns and present findings through clear, data-driven visualizations.

Career focus

I am particularly interested in using data science to uncover patterns, optimize performance, and support strategic decisions. Whether analyzing player performance in sports, exploring financial trends, or solving technical problems, I focus on combining analytical thinking with practical application. I am continuously developing my skills in machine learning, statistical modeling, and data visualization, and I am seeking opportunities to grow as a data scientist in environments that value innovation, impact, and collaboration.
